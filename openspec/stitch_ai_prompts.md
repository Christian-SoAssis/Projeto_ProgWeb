# Stitch AI Design Prompts: Service Marketplace Platform (Dark Mode)

This document contains a series of detailed prompts to be used in Stitch AI for creating a consistent design for the Service Marketplace Platform in a premium Dark Theme.

## 🎨 Visual Identity & Global Style (Standardized)
To maintain consistency across all screens, every prompt follows these design principles:
- **Vibe**: Premium, high-tech, reliable, and cinematic.
- **Color Palette**: 
  - **Primary**: Deep Petrol Blue (#0D3B4C) for surfaces and trust.
  - **Secondary**: Vibrant Mint/Electric Emerald (#2EE5D3) for interaction and CTA.
  - **Neutral**: Deep Charcoal background (#121212) and Light Silver/Off-White (#E2E8F0) for text.
- **Typography**: Clean, geometric sans-serif (like Inter or Montserrat).
- **UI Elements**: Soft rounded corners (16px to 20px), Neomorphic (Soft-UI) surfaces where elements appear to emerge from the dark background using two-tone shadows (lighter grey/charcoal top-left #222222, deep black bottom-right #080808), minimal borders, and generous whitespace.
- **Style**: Dark Neomorphic (Soft-UI) 4k interface, futuristic dashboard aesthetics with tactile, extruded, and inset interactive elements.

## 🚀 1. Hero / Landing Page (PWA Web)
**Context**: First impression for both Clients and Professionals.
**Prompt**:
> Create a high-fidelity mockup of a premium Dark Theme landing page for a service marketplace called "Assist". The design is a PWA (Progressive Web App) optimized for desktop. The header is integrated with the deep charcoal background (#121212) using a subtle neomorphic extrusion effect, featuring a teal logo "Assist" on the left and neomorphic "Login/Register" buttons that appear slightly raised from the surface. The hero section features a bold headline in Portuguese "Conecte-se com profissionais verificados de elite" in light silver. A large neomorphic search bar feels tactile and recessed (inset) into the dark page. Below the search, soft-UI icons for top categories: Home Repair, Legal, IT Support. 4k resolution, dark neomorphic style, modern layout, Brazilian market context.

## 🔍 2. Discovery: Search & Interactive Map
**Context**: Main browsing screen for clients to find professionals.
**Prompt**:
> UI design for a Dark Theme "Search and Discovery" screen of a service marketplace app. The background is deep charcoal (#121212). The screen is split: on the left, a vertical scrollable list of professional cards featuring a soft neomorphic "凸" (convex) effect with subtle #222222 highlights; on the right, an interactive map with custom mint pins. Each card in the list appears as a raised soft surface with the professional's profile picture, name, a "Verified Platinum" badge, specialty, and a 5-star rating. Filter chips are neomorphic toggle buttons that look "凹" (inset) when active. The map uses a dark, vectorized tileset. One map pin is active, showing a small neomorphic popup. Design is elegant, tactile, and uses a Petrol Blue and Vibrant Mint color scheme. 4k resolution.

## 🤖 3. Service Request: AI Image Analysis Flow
**Context**: Client uploading a photo of a problem for AI classification.

> UI/UX design for a Dark Theme "Service Request" flow in a PWA. The screen shows a "Step 2 of 3: Descreva o Problema" phase. Deep charcoal background (#121212). Center stage is a modern Neomorphic "Upload Area" that appears as a large, recessed (inset) container for drag-and-drop. Below the photo, small chips are auto-generated as extruded soft-UI tags in Petrol Blue: "Especialidade: Hidráulica", "Urgência: Alta", "Complexidade: Média". The design is very clean, focusing on trust and technology via tactile depth in the dark. Progressive background, large rounded corner containers (20px), minimalist neomorphic icons. Professional PT-BR interface.

## 💬 5. Client Dashboard & Secure Chat
**Context**: Client managing active jobs and communicating with contractors.
**Prompt**:
> Mobile UI mockup for a Dark Theme "Client Dashboard" and "Secure Chat" screen. Background is deep charcoal (#121212). The interface shows an active conversation where chat bubbles are neomorphic: Dark Petrol Blue for the sender (elevated) and slightly lighter charcoal for the professional (recessed). At the top, a persistent status bar shows "Contrato Ativo" in mint green on a subtle neomorphic surface. A prominent "Confirmar Conclusão e Pagar" button is fixed at the bottom, using a vibrant Electric Mint color with a clear neomorphic click-state (inset vs. extruded). Clean silver typography, 20px rounded corners, extremely high-end tactile feel. PT-BR text.
