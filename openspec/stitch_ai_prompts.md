# Stitch AI Design Prompts: Service Marketplace Platform

This document contains a series of detailed prompts to be used in Stitch AI for creating a consistent design for the Service Marketplace Platform.

## 🎨 Visual Identity & Global Style (Standardized)
To maintain consistency across all screens, every prompt follows these design principles:
- **Vibe**: Professional, reliable, modern, and high-quality.
- **Color Palette**: 
  - **Primary**: Deep Teal/Petrol Blue (#0D3B4C) for trust and authority.
  - **Secondary**: Clean Mint/Soft Emerald (#1EB6A7) for interaction and CTA.
  - **Neutral**: Off-White background (#FAFAFA) and Slate Grey (#334155) for text.
- **Typography**: Clean, geometric sans-serif (like Inter or Montserrat).
- **UI Elements**: Soft rounded corners (12px), subtle glassmorphism on cards, extremely soft shadows, and generous whitespace.
- **Style**: Ultra-realistic 4k interface, minimalist dashboard aesthetics.

## 🚀 1. Hero / Landing Page (PWA Web)
**Context**: First impression for both Clients and Professionals.
**Prompt**:
> Create a high-fidelity mockup of a landing page for a premium service marketplace called "Assist". The design is a PWA (Progressive Web App) optimized for desktop. The header is transparent with a glassmorphism effect, featuring a teal logo "Assist" on the left and "Login/Register" buttons on the right. The hero section features a bold headline in Portuguese "Conecte-se com profissionais verificados de elite" and a search bar centered with two fields: "Qual serviço você precisa?" (e.g., Plumbing, Law) and "Localização". Below the search, small icons for top categories: Home Repair, Legal, IT Support. The background is clean white with a subtle, blurred image of a professional at work. Bottom of the screen shows trust badges: "Profissionais Verificados" and "Pagamento Seguro via PIX". 4k, minimalist, modern layout, Brazilian market context.

## 🔍 2. Discovery: Search & Interactive Map
**Context**: Main browsing screen for clients to find professionals.
**Prompt**:
> UI design for a "Search and Discovery" screen of a service marketplace app. The screen is split: on the left, a vertical scrollable list of professional cards; on the right, an interactive map with custom teal pins. Each card in the list features a professional's profile picture, name, a "Verified Platinum" badge, specialty (e.g., "Mestre de Obras"), and a 5-star rating. Below the header, there are filter chips: "Preço", "Distância", "Mais Bem Avaliados". The map uses a soft, neutral-colored tileset. One map pin is active, showing a small popup with the professional's name and price. Design is elegant, responsive, and uses a Petrol Blue and Mint color scheme. 4k resolution, clean interface.

---

## 🤖 3. Service Request: AI Image Analysis Flow
**Context**: Client uploading a photo of a problem for AI classification.
**Prompt**:
> UI/UX design for a "Service Request" flow in a PWA. The screen shows a "Step 2 of 3: Descreva o Problema" phase. Center stage is a modern "Upload Area" where a user has just uploaded a photo of a leaking pipe. A sleek, semi-transparent overlay shows "AI Analysis in Progress..." with a glowing mint-colored progress ring. Below the photo, small tags are being auto-generated by the AI: "Especialidade: Hidráulica", "Urgência: Alta", "Complexidade: Média". The design is very clean, focusing on trust and technology. Gradient background in soft grey, rounded corner containers, minimalist icons. Professional PT-BR interface.

## 📊 4. Professional Dashboard: Analytics & Agenda
**Context**: The private area for service providers to manage their work.
**Prompt**:
> A premium "Professional Dashboard" interface for a service marketplace. The sidebar is a deep Petrol Blue with minimalist white icons (Home, Bids, Agenda, Earnings). The main content area features three main sections: 1. "Resumo Financeiro" with a large, clean chart showing monthly earnings in BRL. 2. "Agenda" showing a horizontal calendar with active contracts (Client names and times). 3. "Reputação Granular" using a radar chart (spider chart) showing scores for Quality, Punctuality, Communication, and Cleanliness. The overall aesthetic is "Glassmorphism" with soft shadows on white cards. High-contrast typography, very modern and data-driven.

## 💬 5. Client Dashboard & Secure Chat
**Context**: Client managing active jobs and communicating with contractors.
**Prompt**:
> Mobile UI mockup for a "Client Dashboard" and "Secure Chat" screen. The interface shows an active conversation with a professional. The chat bubbles are clean: Petrol Blue for the sender and Soft Grey for the professional. At the top, a persistent status bar shows "Contrato Ativo - Pagamento via Plataforma Garantido". A prominent "Confirmar Conclusão e Pagar" button is fixed at the bottom with a Mint color. The chat features a small yellow "Smart Alert" icon at the top saying "Chat Monitorado para sua Segurança". Clean typography, 12px rounded corners, extremely high-end feel. PT-BR text.
